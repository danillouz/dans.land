Dan's Land

Greetings traveler. I am Dan. The ruler of this domain.

In ages past, I soared through the heavens as the rider of great metal dragons, commanding these magnificent beasts across the vast skies. But in the year of our realm 2012, I heard the calling of a different magic—the ancient art of software crafting.

Now, from The Tower high atop Mount Dan, I serve the Legendary Builders of Framer: as Warlock of the Backend Voids, I cast powerful spells and command vast armies of crazed Gophers to SHAPE THE DIGITAL AETHER TO MY WILL!

When not whispering to TCP/IP packets, you might find me lost in ancient tomes chronicling epic adventures. I am also known to practice the Dark Arts of Pastromancy (the forbidden ritual of consuming questionable amounts of baked goods).

To balance out the baked temptations of evil, I sometimes attempt the sacred dance of salsa—though I must confess, my mastery of this art remains... a work in progress. You may even spot me traversing the cobblestone streets upon my trusted mechanical steed, although the commoners merely call it "bicycle".
